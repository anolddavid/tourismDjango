Welcome to MatukutaTravels

Are you looking for an unforgettable adventure in the heart of Africa?

Do you want to challenge yourself and reach the highest point of the continent? Do you want to experience the diverse and stunning landscapes of Tanzania? If you answered yes to any of these questions, then you are in the right place.

Welcome to MatukutaTravelsTanzania, the best way to explore the highest mountain in Africa and one of the Seven Summits of the world. Whether you are a novice or an experienced climber, we have the perfect package for you. We offer a variety of routes, from the scenic Lemosho to the challenging Umbwe, with professional guides, quality equipment, and ethical porter treatment. We also organize safaris and cultural tours in Tanzania, so you can enjoy the wildlife and the people of this beautiful country. Join us for an unforgettable adventure on Mount Kilimanjaro!

Here are some of our routes

Lemosho route

This is a newer and longer route that approaches from the west. It offers great scenery, high success rates, and less crowds. It takes 7 to 8 days to complete and passes through diverse landscapes, including rainforest, moorland, alpine desert, and glacier.

For $1000
Machame route

This is the most popular and scenic route on Kilimanjaro. It approaches from the south and has a good acclimatization profile, with a climb high, sleep low opportunity on day 3. It takes 6 to 7 days to complete and involves camping in tents.

For $1000
Rongai route

This is the only route that approaches from the north, near the Kenyan border. It is a remote and quiet route that offers a different perspective of Kilimanjaro. It takes 6 to 7 days to complete and involves camping in tents. It has a moderate difficulty level and a good success rate.

For $1000
Umbwe Route

This is the shortest and steepest route on Kilimanjaro. It approaches from the south and has a very rapid ascent profile. It takes 5 to 6 days to complete and involves camping in tents. It has the lowest success rate of all routes due to its extreme difficulty and poor acclimatization.

For $1000
Marangu Route

This is the oldest and easiest route on Kilimanjaro. It approaches from the south-east and has a gradual ascent profile. It takes 5 to 6 days to complete and involves sleeping in huts. It has a lower success rate than other routes due to its short duration and lack of acclimatization.

For $1000
Shira Route

This is a variant of the Lemosho route that starts at a higher altitude on the Shira Plateau. It joins the Lemosho route on day 2 and follows the same path to the summit. It takes 7 to 8 days to complete and involves camping in tents. It has a lower success rate than Lemosho due to its high starting point and less acclimatization time.

For $1000
Northern Circuit Route

This is the newest and longest route on Kilimanjaro. It circles around the mountain from the west to the north-east, covering nearly all sides of Kilimanjaro. It takes 8 to 9 days to complete and involves camping in tents. It has the best acclimatization profile and the highest success rate of all routes.

For $1000

About

Find Out More About Us

Welcome to Kilimanjaro Tours, your ultimate destination for an unforgettable adventure in Tanzania.
We are a team of passionate and experienced guides who have been leading climbers and hikers to the summit of Africa's highest mountain for over 10 years. We offer a variety of routes and packages to suit your preferences, budget, and fitness level. Whether you are looking for a challenging trek, a scenic hike, or a cultural experience, we have something for you.

Why choose us?

  • We have a high success rate of over 90% on all our Kilimanjaro climbs, thanks to our professional and knowledgeable guides who will ensure your safety and comfort throughout the journey.
  • We are committed to responsible tourism and ethical practices, which means we treat our porters and staff fairly, respect the local communities and culture, and protect the environment and wildlife.
  • We provide quality equipment, delicious food, and comfortable accommodation on the mountain and at our base hotel in Moshi town.
  • We are more than just a Kilimanjaro tour company. We also offer amazing safari and Zanzibar extensions that will allow you to explore the rich diversity and beauty of Tanzania.

Join us today and make your dream of reaching the roof of Africa a reality.
Contact us now and let us help you plan your perfect Kilimanjaro adventure and also to let Us plan your routes, prices, dates, gear and preparations.
